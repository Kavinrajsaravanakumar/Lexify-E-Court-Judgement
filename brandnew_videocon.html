Online Court Session

Welcome to the Online Court Session. This platform allows you to participate in court proceedings from the comfort of your home or office. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and a quiet environment for the best experience.

Session Guidelines

  • Join the session at least 10 minutes early to resolve any technical issues.
  • Ensure your camera and microphone are working properly.
  • Dress appropriately as you would for an in-person court appearance.
  • Mute your microphone when not speaking to minimize background noise.
  • Follow the judge's instructions and respect courtroom decorum.

Technical Support

If you encounter any technical difficulties during the session, please contact our support team at support@court.com.
